First Aid Instruction

Learn first basic first aid and help those you love!

What will you learn?

Wound Care

Learn how to properly care for small cuts and scrapes, including avoiding infection, helping healing, and knowing when to go to a doctor.

{{}}

Burns

Learn about different types of burns and how to care for them at home. You need to go to a doctor less often than you think!

{{}}

Fractures

Learn how to recognize an "unstable injury" that needs to go to the emergency department vs a "stable injury" that can be watched.
