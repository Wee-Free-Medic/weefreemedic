Learn Emergency Medical Skills Customized To You

I have more than a decade of experience teaching emergency medical skills to students from all backgrounds. I have been and emergency medical technician for many years and I am a senior medical student at Stanford now, where I teach basic life support and CPR and used to teach wilderness first aid.

I offer some pre-scheduled group classes in CPR and first aid, but the main thing that I offer are customized classes for you and your friends or loved ones to learn together. I can combine CPR (with or without a certification), first aid, and wilderness medicine instruction.

These classes can be done in your home or, particularly for wilderness medicine classes, in outdoor parks in the Bay Area.

Pricing depends on the class and number of students, but starts at around $50 a person for just CPR and goes up to around $200 a person for a full weekend course including multiple elements.

I also offer mass-classes for free, for example for schools. Please contact me if you would like more info.
